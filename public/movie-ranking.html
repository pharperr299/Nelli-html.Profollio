The Best Movie According to Nellie Harper

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Road to Damarcas

This is my number 1 favourite movie. I love how Paul went from prosecuting God to serving him whole heartedly.

The Heart that Forgaves

My favourite christian movie. This movie teaches christians how to live and love again as a single parent in time of sorrow.

Love and Life

We all need a true friend like these 3 womens, i love this film
This movie follows three friends who stick out for one another throgh thick and thin.
It is a story of love, friendship and life.
